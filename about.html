We are here for you! We give our customers a place to rest and relax over great food and drinks made by our passionate team of chefs to ensure each visit is a memorable experience. Our locations are open from 7am to midnight, and have free WIFI. Should you be at home or in the office and can’t make it to your nearest branch, simply call 0721496346 and we’ll bring your favorite delicacies to you through our efficient delivery service. Our Vision, Our Passion Founded in 2014, Rhapsody has maintained its vision of providing high quality and consistency in its food and service. We are driven by our passion for excellence and are constantly growing and improving on our service delivery. We are also proud of our ever-expanding family of over 700 staff who are constantly reaching higher personal and professional goals. It is our firm belief, that by investing in our people, through consistent training, we are lifting the level of expertise in the local industry and by extension, creating a flourishing investment climate for our country.
